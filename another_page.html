Return

Studying webdvelopment is an interesting experience. It can be easy like swimming on a shallow pool, and sometimes things can just flow well. Other times you might get stuck, and it feels like swimming around to nowhere. But, with enough perserverence, like a diver swimming up, a solution will be found and everything can suddenly click. And much like surfing or diving, it requires practice and experience, and to a certain level, enjoyment of the process.
